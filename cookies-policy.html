Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy has been prepared to provide information about the cookies and analytics technologies used when you visit the TamgamCAD website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored in your browser by websites you visit. These files help websites remember preferences, analyze visitor behavior, and improve user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

The following types of cookies may be used on the TamgamCAD website:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of the website.
  • Analytics and Performance Cookies: Used to analyze website usage, measure performance, and improve user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: May be used to remember user preferences and session information.

3. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to analyze visitor statistics and improve website performance.

Google Analytics may process anonymous or aggregated information such as:

  • Visited pages
  • Time spent on pages
  • Click and navigation behavior
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser information
  • Approximate location information
  • Traffic source information
  • Age range
  • Gender
  • Interest categories and demographic information

These data are used only for anonymous, aggregated, and statistical analysis purposes. TamgamCAD does not directly identify users or create personal user profiles.

4. Google Search Console

Our website uses Google Search Console to analyze search engine performance.

This service may provide technical and anonymous statistics such as:

  • Search queries
  • Click-through rates
  • Page performance data
  • Search indexing information

5. Purpose of Data Usage

Collected analytics data are used only for the following purposes:

  • Improving website performance
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Analyzing technical issues
  • Optimizing website content and pages
  • Measuring search engine performance
  • Maintaining website security and stability

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage, delete, or block cookies through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities of the website.

7. Third-Party Services

TamgamCAD may use third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Search Console
  • Google Tag Manager infrastructure
  • EmailJS communication services

Data processing practices of these services are governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Policy Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
