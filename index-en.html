Why TamgamCAD?

📐

Advanced Modeling and Modification

Build your designs flawlessly with industry-standard editing commands. Gain full control over complex geometries with limitless freehand drawing capabilities, seamless polyline creation infrastructure, and dynamic isometric grid support.

🔒

Local Workspace and Data Security

We value your intellectual property. Your drawing files and projects are stored locally directly on your device unless you opt for external cloud synchronization. You are entirely in control.

🖨️

Export in Universal Formats

Export your technical drawings, complete with precise dimensions and custom symbols, in high-resolution DXF, PDF, SVG, and PNG formats. Easily share your 2D CAD projects with lossless vector quality for professional presentations and detailed prints.

👆

Touch and Stylus Support

TamgamCAD is optimized for touch screens and tablets. Zoom and pan the canvas with two fingers, or make millimeter-precise drawings directly on the screen with your stylus.
